Al-‘Aqida Al-Tahawiyyah - Part Of The Sacred Scrolls Series

Noor-uz-Zamaan academy are honoured to hold a two-day course on Al-‘Aqida Al-Tahawiyyah under Shaykh Abul Hasan Hussain Ahmed (Hafidhahullah). A reading of Al-Araba’in Al-Nawawi and Awa’il Al-Sumbuliyya will also be done inshaAllah. Ijaza will granted for all three books Inshallah.

Students (male or female) from across the globe will get the opportunity to benefit immensely from this structured course.
